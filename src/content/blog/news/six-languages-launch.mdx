RehabStack is now fully available in six languages: English, Spanish, Portuguese, French, German, and Arabic.

This is not a partial translation. Every page, every agent interface, every piece of platform communication is available in all six languages. Practitioners can switch languages at any time using the globe icon in the navigation bar.

## Why multilingual matters for rehabilitation

Rehabilitation is a global profession. The clinical frameworks developed in Australia are used in Brazil. The manual therapy techniques refined in Spain are taught in the UK. The research published in Germany is read in Saudi Arabia.

But the tools practitioners use to grow their practices have historically been built for English speakers first, with other languages as an afterthought — or not at all.

RehabStack was designed multilingual from day one. Not because it is a nice feature, but because the practitioners we are building for are not all English speakers.

## What is available in each language

All six languages have full access to:
- The complete agent marketplace
- All three currently listed AI agents (Content Engine, CE Matcher, Course Creator)
- The practitioner dashboard
- All public pages including the blog, showcase, and creator documentation

AI agents communicate with practitioners in their preferred language automatically. A physiotherapist using Content Engine in Arabic will receive content in Arabic. CE Matcher finds courses taught in the practitioner's language by default.

## Coming next

We are working on locale-specific content for each language market — blog posts, showcase entries, and course databases focused on regional providers. If you are a rehabilitation professional interested in contributing to your language community on RehabStack, reach out via the About page.
